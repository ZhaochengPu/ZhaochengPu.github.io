I wanted one quiet place for my projects, notes, and progress in computer science.

The first version of this site is deliberately small: **about, projects, notes, and CV**. It uses the al-folio theme and GitHub Pages, inspired by the clean personal sites common in computer science research.

For now, the goal is not to look accomplished. The goal is to keep a clear record of learning and building—then let the site grow as the work grows.

Next, I plan to add real course projects, short technical notes, a profile photo, and a fuller CV when they are ready.
